Power Interruption Notice - 28.04.2017

Kenya Power will interrupt electricity supply in parts of Mai Mahiu, Mathore, Kimende and Kinale on Friday 5th May, 2017 from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Below are the details of the areas that will be affected during the power interruption period:

Filter

  • North Eastern Region

    Kiambu County AREA: Mai Mahiu, Mathore, Kimende, Kinale Friday 05.05.2017, 9.00 A.M. – 5.00 P.M.

    Ewaso Kedong’, Najire Girls, Karima Girls Sch, Earth Station, Ngema Pumping Stn, Mai Mahiu SGR Substation, Mai Mahiu, Part of Mai Mahiu-Kijabe, Kijabe Hosp, Kirenga, Escarpment, Mbauni, Ewasnarok Rd, Longonot, IDP Camp, Msafiri , Whole of Mathore, Rukuma Centre, Kirenga, Kimende Town, Whole of Kinale, Gitithia, Kambaa, Carbacid, Kijabe Maingi, Kingatwa, Soko Mjinga, Whole of Kamae & adjacent customers.

  • Nairobi North Region

    Nairobi County AREA: Parklands, Westlands, Eastleigh Friday 05.05.2017, 9.00 A.M. – 5.00 P.M.

    Guru Nanak, KIE, Pangani Police Stn, Pangani Girls, Eastleigh High Sch, Pumwani Sec, Starehe Boys, Nomad Hotel, Part of JujaRd, Okoth Aura Rd, Chai Rd, Muratina Rd, Major Kinyanjui Rd, Part of General Waruinge Street, Part of Old Race Course Est & adjacent customers.
